Our Skating Programs

Beginner Skating

Our beginner program is designed for those who are new to skating. Learn the basics of balance, stopping, and turning in a fun and supportive environment.

Intermediate Skating

This program is for skaters who have mastered the basics and are ready to learn more advanced techniques, including jumps and spins.

Advanced Skating

Our advanced program focuses on perfecting skills and preparing for competitions. Skaters will work on choreography and performance techniques.

Figure Skating

This program is tailored for those interested in figure skating. Participants will learn artistic expression, spins, and jumps.
